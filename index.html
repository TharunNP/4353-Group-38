About

The application is designed to predict the rate of fuel based on multiple factors such as client location, history, gallons requested, and company profit margin. It is intended for use by an oil and gas company to offer personalized pricing for their customers. The client location, whether in-state or out-of-state, would impact the pricing due to differences in taxes and regulations. The client history would also be taken into account, as existing customers with previous purchases may receive preferential pricing. The gallons requested would also be considered, as larger orders may receive lower pricing per gallon. Finally, the company's profit margin percentage would be used to calculate the final price. Overall, this application would allow the oil and gas company to provide more accurate and competitive pricing for their customers while also maintaining their profit margins.
